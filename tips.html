Monthly Gardening Tips

  • April: Start vegetable seeds indoors.
  • May: Move seedlings outside after the last frost.
  • June: Add mulch around plants to keep soil moist.
  • July: Water early in the morning to reduce evaporation.
  • August: Remove dead flowers to encourage more blooms.
  • September: Plant cool‑season crops like kale and lettuce.
