About Us

Bloom Valley Nursery started as a small family business in 1990. We grow plants using sustainable methods and sell only healthy, local varieties. Our goal is to help every gardener succeed (Sophia Learning, 2026).

We offer custom orders, free planting advice, and workshops. Visit our nursery to see our full selection.
